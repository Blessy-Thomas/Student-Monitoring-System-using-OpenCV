ATTENTION DETECTION

During the online lectures whenever the students are found doing any other activity away from their laptops or the screens, the system can profoundly understand that the students are not paying attention.The system analyses the attentiveness of the students attending the online lectures. It gives an alert message to the student every time the student is found inattentive during the lecture.

It will be the systems job and students job to be attentive during an online lecture.
